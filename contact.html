Quick answers to common questions

What services does thm Group offer?

thm Group offers three main divisions: thm Graphics (design services), thm Media (video and photography), and thm Solutions (technology and development).

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. Simple projects may take 1-2 weeks, while larger projects can take several months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with clients internationally?

Yes, we work with clients worldwide. We can accommodate different time zones and provide remote consultations and project management.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer flexible pricing based on project scope and requirements. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your specific needs. Contact us for a custom quote.
